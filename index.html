ABOUT US

The Garden is a Sydeny-based online boutique florist, specialising in custom floral arrangements for weddings, special occasions, corporate events, and the everyday. Established in 2019, The Garden is already a major player in the Australian floristry sector. Please read about our key features below:

  • Boutique, custom arrangements that are unique to The Garden.
  • Convenience, customers can place orders 24/7 via the online store.
  • Convenience, arrangements are delivered to the customer’s preferred address, saving time and effort for the customer as they do not need to attend a physical shopfront.
  • Extensive variety of flowers available for purchase.
  • Subscription service reduces the cognitive load for consumers, enabling them to ‘set and forget’.

The Garden sources its flowers from only the best suppliers so that you, the customer, receives the most beautiful flowers at rock bottom prices.
