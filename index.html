ABOUT US

A stylish new cocktail and events bar has opened up in the heart of Kings Cross at the old World Bar. The Wonderland Bar plays host to a killer cocktail list, a beautiful bar and cocktail lounge area as well as our Mad Hatter tea room experience, where you can book a ticket to be taken down the rabbit hole and into a wacky theatrical cocktail making, riddle solving adventure.

Whether you are meeting for after work drinks, having a girls night out, indulging in a special date night or are just looking for a unique and different place to grab a drink, then the Wonderland Bar is where it's at.

Instructions for Doublets

This game was invented by C. L. Dodgson (1832-1898), who (using the pseudonym Lewis Carroll) also wrote Alice's Adventures in Wonderland.

The aim of the game is to get from a given initial word to a given final word by changing only one letter per line. Note that every line must be a valid word. Sometimes there are several possibilities (see example).
